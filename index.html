How it began ?

Creating Whale Coin Talk kind of just happened. There is no simpler way to describe it. One day, I started a group intending to discuss new projects, inviting all my close friends, including LeDizzle. Where did all the friends come from? A whale group, of course, so it only made sense to call the new group “Whale Coin Talk”.

While Whale Coin Talk initially started with a pod of whales, we quickly branched out, welcoming investors of all sizes, from tiny minnows to colossal whales. From the beginning, Whale Coin Talk was your home as long as you maintained an eagerness to learn and treated others with respect. And the embodiment of these principles continues as our membership explodes into the thousands.

Although it feels like a lifetime, the journey is just beginning. I can't wait to see what the future has in store. One thing is for sure; I couldn't do it without this amazing community and irreplaceable team. Thank you for your continued support!
